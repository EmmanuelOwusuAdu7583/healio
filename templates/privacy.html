Ma Nkwa is a research-portfolio pilot for medication adherence and symptom tracking, built to support a small number of doctor-patient pairs in a closed trial. It is not a commercial product.

What we collect

Doctors enter basic clinical information about their patients (diagnosis, medication, treatment duration). Patients enter daily check-ins and weekly symptom reports. This data is stored to power the dashboard, adherence tracking, and automatic risk flagging that doctors use to monitor their patients.

Who can see it

Only the assigned doctor for a given patient, and the pilot administrator, can see a patient's data. Patients can see their own check-ins, reports, and notes their doctor has written.

Account access

Accounts are created by the administrator or a doctor — there is no open sign-up. If you lose your login details, contact your doctor or the administrator directly; see the Contact page.
