Ma Nkwa is provided as a research pilot, not a certified medical device. It is a tool for doctors and patients in the pilot to track adherence and symptoms — it does not replace professional medical judgment or emergency care.

Accounts

Accounts are issued by the administrator (for doctors) or by a doctor (for their patients). You're responsible for keeping your login details private. If your account details are lost, contact your doctor or administrator to have the account reissued — see Contact.

Acceptable use

Use Ma Nkwa only for its intended purpose — tracking your own (or your patients') medication adherence and symptoms. Don't attempt to access accounts or data that aren't yours.

In an emergency

Ma Nkwa is not monitored in real time. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, contact emergency services directly — do not rely on this app.
